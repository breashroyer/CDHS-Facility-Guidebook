Welcome

We've put together this guidebook to assist you during your time at our esteemed Pueblo facility. Whether you're looking for first day instructions, facility amenities, or how-to's on timekeeping, we've got everything you need right here.

Given the current restrictions in place, we understand that having a physical guidebook may not be possible. Therefore, we've created this digital version to ensure that you can navigate the facility and make the most of your assignment.

Here's how to make the most of this Guidebook:

Save This Link: When you access this guidebook on your mobile device, be sure to save this link in your phones notes or text messages or bookmark it in your preferred web browser.

Print or Save a PDF: If you'd like to have certain pages printed out before your assignment or if you prefer to save a PDF copy to your device, simply tap the print button above. It won't print immediately but will generate a PDF file for you to save or print at your convenience.

What's next? Review the First Day Instructions before you start your assignment at Pueblo and review each tab in this guidebook. Welcome to Colorado Mental Health Hospital In Pueblo!

First Day Instructions

Orientation: Approximately 3 weeks depending on discipline (see below)

Week 1 Schedule
Tuesday: 8:00a - 5:00p
Wednesday – Friday: 7:30a – 5:00-5:30p

Week 2 Schedule
Monday – Friday: 7:30a – 5:00-5:30p

Week 3 Schedule
Post Nursing New Employee Orientatation (NEO) with Cara Silla

CNA/CCA/UMHC: 2 days of full performance orientation.

LPN/LVN/HCT: 2 days of full performance orientation with 1 day passing medications on the unit.

RN: 3 days of full performance orientation with at least 1 day passing medications on the unit and at least 1 day performing Charge Nurse duties on the unit.

Note: Official holidays can affect orientation schedule and may bump orientation into the following week 1 or 2 days.

Location For Monday

1600 West 24th Street, Pueblo CO 81003 (from W. 24th Street enter onto CMHHIP campus to the stop sign)

Building #125 - Administration Building (beige building to the left from the stop sign, honeycomb design)

You will need to check in at the guard station and you will meet with our Nursing Education Nurse Manager Cara Silla or Lisa VanCamp to be escorted to orientation.

Phones & Smartwatches

Phones and smartwatches are NOT allowed on the units whatsoever and can result in severe consequences. Phones & smartwatches seen within the units will be confiscated - these items must stay in their lockers.

Dress Code

On your first day casual wear, or scrubs (no white scrubs) are acceptable.

No facial piercings or nails that are more than ¼” long.

Note: You will be provided the Dress code policy during orientation.
